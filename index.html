A Long Lost English Dialect

This page will be dedicated to collating the very few words left that are known in this dialect of English.


chobblesome (adj) - talk-worthy, something that should be talked about

Context: This information is chobblesome! Let me tell Joshua!


gandleband (adj) - messing around, fooling around; not being productive

Other forms: gandlebander (n), gandlebanding (v)

Context: Stop gandlebanding, Joshua!


quixly (adj) - fabulous, amazing; really awesome

Context: Joshua's test score was quixly.


pinter (n) - a stupid person, who believes he is amazing

Other forms: pinty (adj)

Context: Peter Yong is a pinter.


kipplesuss (adj) - the opposite of gandlebanding

Other forms: kipplesusser (n), kippleussing (v)

Context: Joshua needs to stop gandlebanding, and start kipplesussing!


plipcrik (adj) – poppycock, rubbish

Context: "This website is plipcrick!" said Joshua. Those were the last words he ever said.


zonigard (n) – a cheater; someone who does not play by the rules

Context: Aaron was complaining because Joshua was being such a zonigard in their online match of diep.io.


fizzlepop (adj) – something very exciting

Context: Joshua saw a fluffy bird in a tree. He thought it was fizzlepop.


yinglebout (n) – someone who thinks they are very cool, but really they are not

Context: William Bodycote is a huge yinglebout.


quildittle (n) – something that goes very fast; something very quick

Context: I was terrified by Joshua's quildittle car, which almost ran me over.


triplerfied (n) – a real fatso; someone large

Context: "I think the word triplerfied is a perfect word to describe you." said Joshua. Those were his true last words.


bilgrich (n) – someone or something really bad

Context: I would almost certainly describe William Bodycote as a bilgrich.


odraft (adj) – a word to describe something cold

Context: In winter, the air is always odraft, don't you think Joshua?


pultrikide (adj) – a word to describe something hot

Context: Joshua thought that the girl across the road was pultrikide.
